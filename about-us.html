About Us

our vision

East London’s ethnic minorities are financially and socially prosperous.

our mission

To make East London’s ethnic minorities financially and socially prosperous by maximising their income, consolidating their role as active citizens, and connecting them to life-changing oppor tunities.

our values

Our values form the acronym G.R.O.W.T.H – a reflection of our commitment to fostering development and progress in our community.
