A PROSPEROUS FUTURE

Building The Limehouse Project

A Community's Vision

In the heart of East London, a story of community-driven change and empowerment began in 1984. The Limehouse Project (LHP) was born not from distant boardrooms or external agencies but from the local community it sought to serve. Founded by visionary locals who understood our diverse community's unique challenges and potential, LHP is a testament to the power of community action.

THE COMMUNITY TREE

From Local Roots to Widespread Branches

LHP's journey started with a simple yet powerful objective: to address the immediate housing needs of homeless families in our area. Spearheaded by three local social pioneers – a teacher, a councillor, and a social worker – the organisation quickly evolved. Recognising the broader issues faced by ethnic minorities, including financial difficulties, social isolation, and language barriers, LHP expanded its focus. Today, we offer a suite of services ranging from benefits advice to employment ranging from benefits advice to employment
