The Board

Our Board of Trustees, a collective of local leaders and seasoned professionals, guides LHP with strategic wisdom and deep community insight. Their governance ensures that our mission remains focused and our actions consistently align with our goals.

Our current board of trustees is composed of eight people who bring diverse skills and a deep commitment to Limehouse Project's values and mission. The professional expertise includes former teachers, school governors, employment advisers, risk management consultants, corporate lawyers, barristers, financial sector representatives and community advisors.
